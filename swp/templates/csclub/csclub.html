Hackerspace

The Hackerspace at Chapel Hill, usually referred to as HatCH, meets every Thursday at 7:30 in Sitterson 027. Members of HatCH are inspired to create a friendly and open community of students interested in participating in various DIY projects involving electronics and art. Come to our weekly gatherings to learn about technology, collaborate on technical ventures, work on individual projects, or just relax and enjoy the company of our HatCHlings! Contact Roman Myers for further inquiries about how awesome we are. Or, join our Facebook group!
